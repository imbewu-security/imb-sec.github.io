Residential Estate Security.

WHAT MAKES ESTATE SECURITY EFFECTIVE?Depending on the size of the residential property, Imbewu Specialised Security will assess various requirements, including:

  • The number of entrances and exits
  • The number of homes on the property
  • The height of the walls around the estate
  • The facilities on the property
  • The location

Once these assessments have been made, We will provide an estate security recommendation, made up of various security elements. These include:

  • Amount of guarding staff required
  • CCTV
  • Electric fencing
  • Alarm systems and panic buttons
  • Offsite monitoring

Whatever the size of the residential property, estate security is only as good as the company and staff providing the services.

On all our Residential estates we install a biometric fingerprint reader that records all domestic workers coming and going. This device can be used to exclude entrance if someone no longer works in the estate, at no cost to you.

Our Guards

Our guards are trained to perform beyond the normal with regards to residential estates, as we consider this as a high value target as it is people’s homes and most precious belongings that are been protected.

We install 4 cameras in and around the guardroom, this is a tool that we use to monitor our guards. We stream this data to our control room and monitor the productivity, neatness and performance of the guards.

The guard is tracked by GPS at all times as he/she patrols the property, the estate is equipped with a guard patrol monitoring system that forces the guard to randomly patrol the estate.
